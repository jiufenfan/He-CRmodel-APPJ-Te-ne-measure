# Known Limitations

- v0.1.0 is a scaffold, not a full quantitative reproduction of Lee 2020.
- External primary-source data from Goto, Ralchenko, Belmonte, Santos, Royal/Orel, NIST, and LXCat is not imported.
- Air chemistry is not included.
- J-resolved fine structure is not included.
- Unverified or OCR-uncertain data is disabled by default.
- A project-wide unit/species convention document has not been created yet.
- The solver does not yet parse reaction stoichiometry or assemble full CR production/loss equations.
